Key standards

Visual physical commonsense (V-PC)

  • A single hiker is shown shouting across the open space.
  • The setting is a mountain valley or canyon with the opposite cliff or ridge clearly visible.

Audio physical commonsense (A-PC)

  • A quieter and duller copy of the shout returns in the audio after a clearly perceptible delay.
  • The returning sound is a discrete copy of the original shout's content with reduced amplitude and dulled high frequencies.

Cross-modal physical commonsense (AV-PC)

  • The original shout onset aligns in time with the visible shouting action of the hiker.
  • A discrete delayed echo of the shout follows the original shout in the audio track.
